Pan Fried Salmon

This is a very easy and yummy salmon recipe for 2. I usually serve it with basmati rice and salad. Perfect for during the week.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon mild curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 pinches ground ginger
  • 2 (4 ounce) salmon fillets
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine cream, curry powder, lemon juice, and ginger in a skillet and bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and slide salmon into the cream. Cover and simmer until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 12 minutes depending on the thickness of the fillets.
  2. Flip 1 to 2 times while cooking. Season with salt and pepper.
